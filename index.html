Our Story

Born from a passion
for artisan craft

Noir Delicacies was born from a mother's instinct — the belief that indulgence should never come at the cost of what goes into your body. Every product is crafted in small batches using high-quality ingredients while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and freshness.

We are proud to introduce a dedicated range of wholesome snacks crafted especially for toddlers. As a mother, our founder follows one simple principle: if she wouldn't serve it to her own child, it doesn't make its way into our kitchen.

Our Promise

Clean ingredients.
No compromises.

We read every label, question every supplier, and refuse every shortcut. Because we believe luxury and health are not opposites — they belong on the same plate.

🌿
No Artificial Colours

We use natural hues from beetroot, turmeric, matcha, and fruit — vibrant, safe, and real.

🚫
Zero Preservatives

Every batch is made fresh to order. No stabilisers, no shelf-life chemicals — just honest freshness.

🧈
Real Butter & Cream

We never substitute with margarine or hydrogenated fats. Pure dairy, the way it should be.

🍯
Natural Sweeteners

Unrefined cane sugar, jaggery, honey, and dates — sweetened mindfully, never excessively.
